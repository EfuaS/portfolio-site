About the Project

Storefront was the result of my first course taken in web development in my third year of school and thus, my first interaction with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 4, PHP and Mysql. The focus of this project was to better understand Bootstrap 4 and practice CRUD (Create Retrieve Update Delete) operations in a web application. This is where my interest and passion for development sparked and i dare say that spark is a burning flame today.
